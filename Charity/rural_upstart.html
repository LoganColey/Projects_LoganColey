Mission Statement

Rural Upstart offers jobs from many diffrent orgainsations and compaines to provide a stable career for communities in need. By approaching compaines we convince them to bring their resources to those communities to and help those struggling to get back on their feet.

Goals:

  1. Bring jobs to rural communities with aid from compaines that want to help the communities
  2. Do public outreach to familes in the community to help them offer them jobs that are being brought them in
  3. Tailor the jobs that are need for each indivual in the community
